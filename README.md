# CTF Writeups — Rayan Mejri (bangaalderson)

Hey, I'm bangaalderson, a cybersecurity enthusiast and CTF player from Tunisia.
I enjoy breaking things, understanding how they work under the hood,
and occasionally building challenges that make others suffer (in a fun way).

## What's this repo?

This is my personal collection of CTF writeups — both for challenges
I solve and challenges I create myself.

I don't just want to dump a working exploit and call it a day.
The goal here is to share my full thought process: how I approached
the problem, what I tried, what failed, and how I eventually got there.

Maybe you solved the same challenge differently that's exactly the point.
A different perspective might give you a new trick, a new reflex,
or just a new way of looking at a problem. That's what I'm here for.
